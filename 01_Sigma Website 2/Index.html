Know About Sigma

Welcome to Sigma Mortgage, your trusted partner in private lending solutions. With a commitment to providing flexible, tailored financial solutions, we serve both mortgage brokers and borrowers across Ontario, British Columbia, Alberta and Nova Scotia. Whether you’re seeking short-term financing or unconventional mortgage options, we offer competitive and efficient lending services that go beyond traditional banks.

Who We Are

At Sigma, we are a team of experienced professionals with deep expertise in the Canadian real estate market and private lending. Our approach is simple: we listen to your needs and craft a financing solution that aligns with your goals. Whether you're facing time constraints, need a custom solution for an unconventional property, or navigating complex financial situations, we’re here to help.
